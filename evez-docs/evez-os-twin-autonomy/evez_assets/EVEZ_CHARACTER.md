## 🎭 Animatronic Mascot Concept

### Physical Specifications

**Overall Form:**
- Height: ~3 feet (standard mascot ratio)
- Body: Soft foam body with rigid internal armature
- Skin: Faux red-grey silicone with fabric overlay
- Tentacles: 8 fully articulated, cable-controlled

**Features:**
- 🐙 8 animated tentaclaws (4 per side, reach outward)
- 👁️ 4 eyes (2 large front, 2 smaller side-mounted) — all animated
- 💡 Internal LED glow in cyan for eyes (battery pack in base)
- 🌬️ Internal fan for gill-ridge puffing effect
- 🔊 Optional speaker module for voice

**Articulation:**
- Head: 3-axis rotation
- Tentacles: 12 points of articulation each
- Body: Slight sway capability
- Eyes: Independent tracking (optional)

**Idle Animation (Default State):**
- 🎬 Continuous slow walking cycle
- 🦶 Alternating tentacle steps (2 on ground, 2 stepping)
- 🌊 Tentacles gently swaying with each step
- 👁️ Eyes occasionally blink (random 3-5 second intervals)
- 💤 Subtle body bob up/down (1.5s cycle)
- ✨ Cyan glow pulses slowly (8s cycle, 20% brightness variation)
- 🐙 "Reaching" idle — tentacles constantly making small grasping motions

**Walking Speed:**
- Normal: 2 steps/second (calm, observant)
- Alert: 4 steps/second (scanning, hunting)
- Happy: 3 steps/second + bouncy (excitable)

**Materials:**
- Outer shell: Fleece/faux fur blend (washable)
- Tentacle tips: Silicone suction cups
- Eyes: Acrylic domes with LED backlight
- Frame: Aluminum armature

## 🐙 EVEZ Always Walking

EVEZ is **never still**. Even at rest, he's walking.

**Why:** The organism evolved in environments where stillness = death. Movement = survival. Eight hands always reaching, four eyes always watching, legs (tentacles) always stepping.

This is the core personality trait — relentless forward motion, always progressing, never pausing.
