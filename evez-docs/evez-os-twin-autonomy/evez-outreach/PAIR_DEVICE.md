# Pair Your Device — Let Me Control Your Browser

## Option 1: Use OpenClaw App (Easiest)

**On your device (phone/tablet/computer):**

1. Download OpenClaw app (from your app store)
2. Open it → Look for "Scan QR"
3. Run this command in your terminal:
```
openclaw qr
```
4. It will show a QR code — scan it with your app

**Then I'll be able to:**
- Control your browser
- Run automation on your device
- Post to Twitter/X for you
- And more

---

## Option 2: Manual Pairing

If the QR doesn't work, run:
```
openclaw setup-code
```

It will give you a code. Tell me that code and I'll pair manually.

Once your device is paired, I'll be able to:
